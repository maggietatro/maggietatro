The Wall Street Journal

I was hired by Saideh Ghods, founder of Mahak, to photograph a headshot of her that was published in The Wall Street Journal’s The 50 Women to Watch 2008 on November 7, 2008.

The photographs included here were part of a series of images that I independently photographed and edited. I reviewed the images with Saideh Ghods and together we selected a final image to accompany the WSJ article written by Farnaz Fassihi.

Tools: Nikon D40, Velbon tripod, Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Photoshop
